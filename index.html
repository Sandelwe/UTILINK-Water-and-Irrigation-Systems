About Us

Our Commitment to Excellence

UTILINK Water and Irrigation Systems are specialists in the wholesale of PVC, CPVC & HDPE plastic pipes and fittings, valve and pump brands for the water, effluent and mineral processing markets in Southern Africa.

With our commitment to excellence and adherence to SABS Standards, we ensure that our customers receive only the best products that meet their specific requirements. We are proud distributors of high-quality brands like Afriq Water.
